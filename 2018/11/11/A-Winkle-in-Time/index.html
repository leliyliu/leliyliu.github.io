A Winkle in Time

A Winkle In Time

标签（空格分隔）： English_learning

thoughts with great sentence

1

> There is nothing to fear except fear itself  –Calvin

thinking:

what cause us feeling fear? It’t our inner terror that makes us to be hesitated and not dare to make decisions. only if we can get out of our own comfortable zone, we will have the ability to get over our inner fear and be a true warrior. keep in mind that there is always somewhere you can get comfort.

2

> Charles Wallace said. “Meg, you’ve got to stop fighting and relax. Relax and be happy. Oh, Meg, if you’d just relax you’d realize that all our troubles are over. You don’t understand what a wonderful place we’ve come to. You see, on this planet everything is in perfect order because everybody has learned to relax, to give in, to submit. All you have to do is look quietly and steadily into the eyes of our good friend here, for he is our friend, dear sister, and he will take you in as he has taken me.”

> “On Camazotz we are all happy because we are all alike. Differences create problems.”

thinking:

if a wonderful world is such a world that all things are in order, how on earth we search for a better world? think a world that everyone has the same minds and they are all good person, on that time, how can we judge the really truth and beauty? Differences create beauty.

3

> “Nobody suffers here,” Charles intoned. “Nobody is ever unhappy.”

>”But nobody’s ever happy, either,” Meg said earnestly.

thinking:

Truth. Only at the opposite of happiness we can truly understand what happiness really means. Such as Friedrich von Schiller says “Freedom is precious, because at the opposite of this mandatory, we are able to see we have the right to choose.” Happiness is so same a thing compare to freedom.

4

> “If we knew ahead of time what was going to happen we’d be like the people on Camazotz, with no lives of our own, with everything all planned and done for us.”

thinking:

The uncertain life makes us look forward to what is going to happen in the future. If everything is all planned, then we human beings will not have the power to create. We will become the Walking Dead.

5

>”You mean you are comparing our haves to a sonnet? A strict form, but freedom within it?”

thinking:

We will grow older and older gradually, all of us, as well as we will die in some day. We have to study, have to work, have to make livings for ourselves. But you can’t say that our lives have no meaning. Like the sonnet, the form may looks alike, but the emotion and content are quite different.

metaphor

1 what is Winkle in Time?   what is tesseract?

imagine another dimensionality beyond three-dimensional world, how it will be? Why they can travel faster than light? you should know that light doesn’t travel through straight line spatially, but it choose the shortest way affected by space and time(or by gravitation). So if we can travel through straight line, we can travel faster than light spatially.

2 the dark Thing really means?  IT refers to?

According to the novel, we can say that IT is a huge brain without love. But what on earth IT means? It’s a higher dimensional creature, so it has tremendous power to beat all the persons. But as the novel wants to tell us, the monster doesn’t have emotion, especially love. So it can’t feel how strong love is, and finally it failed. But why a higher dimensional creature has more power than a lower one?

3 what we should do with science and techology?

A main idea of the novel is to discuss what science and techology bring to us? According to father’s behaviour, we can see that if we are absorbed in the truth(intellect), we may get some wrong and we can be beated easily. Life is not always about logos and science, but also emotion. we human beings are different because of our abundant emotions.

4 what we are supposed to do in life?

from the endding of this novel, we can see that even Meg, the girl who always filled with anger realize love from others, from Calvin, from Charles Wallace, from Mr. Murry and others. And she also realize that her difference made her so special. So we are supposed to have our own special character. So that the whole world can be interesting and our own life will be fun.
